# Fundamental Metrics

Fundamentals are regime filters. They do not directly trigger rebalances.

## Daily/weekly Solana metrics

- DeFi TVL
- DEX volume
- Chain fees
- App revenue
- Stablecoin supply
- Active addresses
- Transaction count
- SOL market cap
- SOL/BTC and SOL/ETH relative trend
- Network health / outage risk

## CLMM-specific metrics

- Pool TVL
- Pool 24h volume
- Fee APR trend
- Current tick
- Liquidity near current price
- Position lower/upper bounds
- Distance to lower/upper bound
- Unclaimed fees
- Inventory split: SOL vs USDC
- Estimated IL versus HODL

## Decision use

Fundamentals may adjust:

- Range posture: aggressive / neutral / defensive
- Rebalance sensitivity
- Maximum deployment percentage
- Whether to pause during abnormal risk

Fundamentals may not directly decide:

- Exact ticks
- Swap amount
- Liquidity amount
- Transaction submission
